# Johns Hopkins Research Methods Workshop

Johns Hopkins researchers will host a research methods workshop for graduate students on May 22 at 6 p.m. ET. The session will focus on study design, data documentation, and practical ways to share findings with nontechnical audiences.

The workshop is open to students, faculty, and staff. Participants will review examples from recent healthcare studies, practice writing plain-language summaries, and leave with a checklist for preparing research updates for a website or newsletter.

The event will take place in Gilman Hall. Registration closes May 15.

For questions, contact the program team before the registration deadline.
