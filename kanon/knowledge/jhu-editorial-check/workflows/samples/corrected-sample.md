# Johns Hopkins Innovation Program

Johns Hopkins University will launch a program for local educators focused on practical uses of AI in teaching and research communication.

The Center for Digital Learning will offer workshops, faculty-led discussions, and hands-on planning sessions for participants who want to apply new tools in their classrooms.

Visit the website homepage at noon ET for a historic campuswide lecture titled The Future of Discovery. The lecture will take place in Baltimore, Maryland, and online.
